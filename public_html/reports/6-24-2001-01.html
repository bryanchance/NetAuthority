Net Authority Contest Update

June 24, 2001

We are sorry to report that, due to a technical error, any entries into the contest prior to today were not logged. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. No points will be awarded for entries made prior to June 24. The offenders that were reported prior to this date were counted in our databases, however, so we ask that you please do not re-submit offenders in order to regain your points.

The contest will still end on July 30th, and all of the rules and conditions detailed on the contest page still apply.

Thank you for understanding. God bless.
