Net Authority Server Downtime

June 30, 2001

Due to technical difficulties and security issues, Net Authority was forced to take it's web server offline for the past several days. There is no need to worry, however, as the databases were not compromised in any way. Net Authority asks that you please now submit any persons in violation of the NA-AIUG that you were unable to previously as a result of the downtime.

Net Authority apologizes for any inconvenience the downtime may have caused, and thanks you for your patience.
